channel](3rd_youtube_channel "wikilink") account on March 29, 2018. It

## Meaning of Title

  - "COEF" is a common abbreviation for "coefficient", a
    "[multiplicative factor in some term of a polynomial, a series or
    any expression](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coefficient)".
